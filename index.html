Our Story

Why make Helicopter Conversion Kits for Cars? Roadways are one of the biggest issues today, especially in America. Millions of miles of roads take up so much land, and more and more is taken every day. People wait hours in traffic jams producing pollutants and noise. Above everything else, it can be unsafe to drive, especially if you are tired. Thats why our founders Jameson, Dylan and Anthony came together (and totally not because it's a huge project that we had to do) and began developing the helicopter conversion kit for cars. Although still in its infancy, our product shows incredible promise. Our thought proccess was, why not use what everyone already has, and make it better? That's why we created SwagTech, Driven to Fly.
